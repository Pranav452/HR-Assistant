HR POLICY: VACATION TIME

All full-time employees are entitled to vacation time based on their length of service:

- 0-1 years: 10 days per year
- 1-5 years: 15 days per year  
- 5+ years: 20 days per year

Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.

Unused vacation time may be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 5 days.

For questions about vacation policies, contact HR at hr@company.com or call (555) 123-4567.
